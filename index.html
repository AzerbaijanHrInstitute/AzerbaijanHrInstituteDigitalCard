Azerbaijan HR Institute is the premier learning and development organization for the human resources profession. We provide research, education and representation to promote the standing of the HR profession and engender management best practices within workplaces through our commitment to the development of human capital in Azerbaijan.

Azerbaijan HR Institute offers:
- HR certification & training programs
- Leadership & Management courses
- HRCI preparation courses
- Strategic HR consulting
- Mentoring programs
- E-learning system (LMS)
- HR & Management global events
Education opportunities provided by Azerbaijan HR Institute are aligned with the international standards, capabilities and behaviors of the HR profession. The world's leading credential organizations HRCI (HR Certification Institute) and SHRM (Society for Human Resources Management) provide recertification credits (PDCs) to our educational programs.
